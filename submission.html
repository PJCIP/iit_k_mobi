Submission

Participants who would like to be considered for an oral or a poster presentation shall submit an abstract for the reviewing process.
You need to create an account and submit the abstract.
Then, you can check the status of your abstract in MYPAGE.
The number of poster presenters may be limited depending on the submission status.

Recommendation for publication

Selected papers among full length paper after review will be recommended for publication in Special Issues of following Journals.

1. Chinese Journal of Aeronautics
2. Journal of Mechanical Science and Technology
3. Journal of Propulsion and Energy
4. Journal of Thermal Science
5. International Journal of Gas Turbine, Propulsion and Power Systems
6. International Journal of Fluid Machinery and Systems

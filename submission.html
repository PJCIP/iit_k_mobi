Submission guidelines

The following are instructions for the uploading process:
1. Kindly log in to your Microsoft CMT profile and click on “Asian Congress on Gas Turbines (ACGT-2024)” under the “All Conferences” tab.
2. There, “Select Your Role” as “Author” at the top of the page to access the “Author Console” – this page will list details of the paper(s)            submit to ACGT-2024.
3. Under the “Actions” category, access the “Edit Submission” option: this page should contain abstract submission”:
4. Clicking “Save Changes” at the bottom of the “Edit Submission” page will complete the upload process.
